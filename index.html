NLP - Topic Modeling

Topic Modeling of E-Commerce Reviews

Jun 2026

Mined 5,400 Indonesian Tokopedia reviews (PRDECT-ID) to uncover what drives praise versus complaints. Benchmarked BERTopic (embeddings + UMAP + HDBSCAN) against LDA and K-Means baselines, modeled separately on positive and negative reviews. BERTopic led on coherence and diversity, surfacing 8 praise and 9 complaint themes with actionable business recommendations.

Generative AI - LLM & RAG

FinChat - Chat with SEC 10-K Filings

Jul 2026

A hybrid Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot that answers questions about 25 major companies' SEC 10-K filings, grounded in the source with citations. It fuses semantic text retrieval with each filing's structured XBRL financial data and Python-computed ratios, so it handles both business questions and numeric ones like "What is Apple's quick ratio?". Built on LangChain, ChromaDB and Llama 3.3 70B (Groq) and evaluated with an LLM-as-judge, the hybrid design more than doubled its score on the FinanceBench benchmark - from 20% to 50%.
